# AINOTE Role Context

Use this context for all simulated roles.

AINOTE is an iFLYTEK AI productivity paper tablet / smart office notebook product line for meetings, interviews, study, project collaboration, and mobile work.

Core product traits:

- E Ink paper-like reading and handwriting.
- Real-time speech transcription.
- AI meeting summaries and structured notes.
- AI search, chat, and writing assistance.
- Multi-language meeting and translation scenarios.
- Calendar / schedule workflows.
- Cloud sync and cross-device access.
- Privacy, permission, and data-security sensitivity.

Role-specific attention:

- Product: meeting workflow, note organization, AI output boundaries, copy clarity, cross-device journey, and tablet/device landscape behavior when the target product feature involves horizontal use.
- User: meeting focus, low-interruption operation, trust in transcription and summary quality, paper-like intuition.
- Engineering: recording state, transcription pipeline, speaker separation, OCR, AI summary stability, sync conflict, weak network, cost.
- Leader: market differentiation, AI service cost, hardware/software delivery rhythm, overseas growth, brand trust.
